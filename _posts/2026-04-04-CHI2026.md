I'll be at the upcoming ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems in Barcelona from the 13th to the 17th of April!

Paper Presentation
======
At the conference, I'll be presenting my paper "**Design Explorations of Instruments and Interactions with Bidirectional Haptic Couplings**" on Wednesday the 15th in the "Co-Design in Motion & High-Stakes Contexts" paper session (11:15AM to 12:45PM in P1 - Room 111, my talk is at 12:03PM). You can read all about this work [here](https://davisonaudio.github.io/chi2026/).


Workshops
======
Additionally, I'll be presenting and participating at the following two workshops:

Tuesday 14/04: [Are We Doing Enough? Sharing Design and Manufacturing Expertise Across Open Source Hardware Communities](https://are-we-doing-enough-workshop.github.io/). 
My position paper for this workshop is titled **Development of Open-Source Hardware for Digital Musical Instrument Design**.
Read more it (and download the PDF) [here](/workshops_wip_demo/2026-CHI-awde).

Thursday 16/04: [AI for Haptics and Haptics for AI: Challenges and Opportunities](https://derikon.github.io/HapticsAI_Workshop/). 
My position paper for this workshop is titled **Haptics for Bidirectional Interaction with AI Musical Mappings**.
Read more it (and download the PDF) [here](/workshops_wip_demo/2026-CHI-haptics4ai).
