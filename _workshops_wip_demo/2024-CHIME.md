This was a demo at the [CHIME](https://www.chime.ac.uk/) 2024 annual conference at the Open University in Milton Keynes, UK.


# Abstract:
The haptic instruments presented use a novel method of self-sensing to provide bidirectional tactile interaction. A single voice coil transducer can be used for exciting resonant synthesis models while simultaneously providing vibrotactile feedback to the musician.
